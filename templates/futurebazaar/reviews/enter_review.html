How to write that awesome review!
The basics

Keep it simple, clean, concise and as far as possible, unbiased. Your focus on product features will help customers make an informed decision.
Mention what you liked/disliked and more importantly, why. "Would I like it too?" is a question on top of every customers’ mind and this would help immensely .

Now make it great

A good title catches the eye. Shorter paras makes it more readable. Quick spell check will go a long way (we all hate typos, don't we?).

What we will publish

We'll publish product-specific reviews, both positive and critical, after moderation. In case your writeup contains product review as well as feedback for our service, we will use our discretion. We are committed to providing our customers with most relevant product information.

What we won't

Inflammatory, profane or lewd language is a strict no. Please don't use copyrighted material in your review. Feedbacks/comments for our service, both positive or negative, but unrelated to the product can be sent to us using the “Feedback” tab on the right. We love to hear from our customers!
