Team

Kashyap Deorah
President

After returning to India in Nov 2007, Kashyap founded Chaupaati Bazaar - phone pe deal - to enable Indian consumers to purchase products and services by phone. In Sep 2010, Chaupaati merged into India's largest retailer Future Group.

Prior to Chaupaati, Kashyap started the US office of Geodesic (NSE: 503699) in the capacity of VP, Business Development doing online affiliate sales in North America and mobile OEM sales in Taiwan & China. Kashyap was an early employee at Jasper Design Automation. Besides naming the company, he made contributions to the sales campaigns for key customers and prospects in North America and Northern Europe.

While a final year student at IIT Bombay, Kashyap co-founded Righthalf, the first campus start-up as part of the IIT Bombay Business Incubator. After a successful acquisition of Righthalf by Stratify, he moved to the Silicon Valley in late 2000. Since then, he has worked with high-tech startups doing sales, business development and product management.

Kashyap graduated from IIT Bombay in 2000 with a B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering.

Dhimant Bakshi
Category

Dhimant Started his career with Shoppers' Stop as a Retail Management Trainee. With over 17 years of Retail Experience across various functions such as Buying and Merchandising, Retail Operations, Brand Development, Advertising and Public Relations; he is now on a new page of his career with Ecommerce.

Prior to Futurebazaar.com, Dhimant has been associated with Shoppers' Stop, Globus, Big Bazaar and Reliance Retail. Dhimant is also associated with RAI (Retailers Association of India) as an Industry Speaker. Because of his keen involvement with Education in Retail Education, he is often invited to institutes such as MICA, NMIMS, Welingkar Institute of Management, IGTC as a visiting faculty. He has also a co-authored various educational initiatives for RAI.

Dhimant is a Statistics graduate with Post Graduate in Marketing from Welingkar Institute of Management Studies. Travelling & Photography are two of his most favourite interests. He has received many Best Performer Awards in his career stint and was recently on the panel of 'Shooting Stars, Retail Success Stories' conducted by RAI.

Gaurav Agarwal
Marketing

Gaurav has led many roles in early stage to growth phase starups often working in companies with less 30 employees. His last stint was with Amar Chitra Katha where he created the New Media Division from scratch and grew the spread of ACK Comics to all platforms. Prior to ACK, he has worked in the Microfinance Technology sector and even attempted a startup in the complex event processing space.

Gaurav graduated from IIT Bombay in 2003 with an B. Tech and M. Tech. in Micro Electronics

Krishna Raghavan
Engineering

Starting with his first initiative in '99, Krishna has been involved in product technology organizations across US and India. Krishna has over a decade of experience within the e-commerce domain successfully leading product technology efforts.

As part of Vendio Inc, he led a global product engineering organization to develop a platform for seller storefronts along with the most comprehensive set of tools and services for power sellers to sell on marketplaces like eBay, and Amazon. Vendio was later acquired by Alibaba to power their e-commerce business in North America.

Other than his technology interests, Krishna is a certified Yoga Instructor and is very interested in Indian Philosophy and Mythology.

Krishna has completed the Bachelors Dual Degree Program in Mathematics, Computer Science from Denison University, US in 1999

Zishaan Hayath
Product

Zishaan co-founded Chaupaati Bazaar, a phone-commerce startup that enables India's 500 million mobile users to buy home appliances, children's products and magazines over the phone. In Sep 2010, Chaupaati was acquired by Future Group, India's largest retailer.

Earlier, Zishaan worked with Opera Solutions on strategy and operations consulting projects for Fortune 100 clients in North America, Europe and Asia. He was the first employee in Opera's New Delhi office.

Zishaan graduated from IIT Bombay in 2005 where he was awarded the Institute Citation and two Institute Colors. He is an avid traveler and an amateur photographer.
