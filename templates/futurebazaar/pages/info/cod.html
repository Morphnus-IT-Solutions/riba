What is Cash on Delivery?

Cash on Delivery option allows you to pay cash against the delivery at your shipping address, rather than paying in advance.

How do I place a Cash-on-Delivery Order?

  1. All items that have the "Cash-on-Delivery" Icon are valid for order by Cash-on-Delivery
  2. Add the item(s) to your cart and proceed to checkout.
  3. When prompted to choose a Payment Option, select "Pay By Cash-on-Delivery"
  4. Create a separate cart for products available on Cash-on-Delivery option. (Please create a separate cart for products not available on "Cash-on-Delivery")
  5. When prompted, please verify your contact number. Our customer service executive will call you on the number provided to confirm your order.
  6. Once verified and confirmed, your shipment will be processed in the time specified, from the date of confirmation.
  7. You will be required to make a Cash payment to our Courier Partner, only at the time of delivery of your order.

Terms & Conditions

  • You will get this payment option only if your shipping address is serviceable for Cash-on-Delivery and order value less than Rs. 18,000.
  • Please make ONLY Cash Payment at the time of delivery. Cheques, Demand Drafts or any other payment mode will not be entertained
