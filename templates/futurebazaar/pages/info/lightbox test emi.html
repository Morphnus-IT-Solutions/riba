HDFC Credit Card Equal Monthly Installment (EMI)

  1. We offer our customers the facility to avail of EMI's while shopping online.
  2. HDFC Bank Credit Card customers can avail of the EMI facility.
  3. You can choose to pay in installments of 3, 6 or 9 months.
  4. Please note that you will be charged processing fees. HDFC 1.95% / 4.2% / 6.85% for 3/6/9 EMI.
  5. Minimum purchase value required for availing EMI: Rs. 3,000.
