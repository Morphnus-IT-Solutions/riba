EMI ICICI & HDFC Credit Card Equal Monthly Installment (EMI)

  1. We offer our customers the facility to avail of EMI's while shopping online.
  2. ICICI & HDFC Bank Credit Card customers can avail of the EMI facility.
  3. You can choose to pay in installments of 3, 6 or 9 months.
  4. Please note that you will be charged processing fees. ICICI - 2.9% / 5.9% and 8.9% for 3/6/9 EMI and HDFC 1.95% / 4.2% / 6.85% for 3/6/9 EMI.
  5. Minimum purchase value required for availing EMI: Rs. 3,000.

Please note that following set of customers are NOT eligible for the service:

  • ICICI Bank Amex Credit Cardholders
  • ICICI Bank EMI Credit Cardholders
  • ICICI Bank Corporate Credit Cardholders
  • ICICI Bank Business Cardholders
  • ICICI Central Gold Card
  • ICICI Bank Virtual Cards
