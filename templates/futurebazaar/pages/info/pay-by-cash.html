Payment Instructions

How to pay using EasyBill?

  • Find the store nearest to you using the Easy Bill store locator.
  • Note down your Order Number and amount payable.
  • Once you are at the EasyBill outlet, you will need to mention that you want to make a payment for a Futurebazaar order.
  • Hand over the specified amount of cash to the person manning the EasyBill counter.
  • You will get a printed Easybill receipt with the order number, amount, date and other details. Kindly retain this slip with you.
  • We will typically approve your order on the next working day and your shipment should reach you within the stipulated time frame.
  • Please note that most EasyBill counters are placed within shops and similar establishments.

  • ATM / Cash deposit machines should NOT be used for this purpose
  • In case you need the exact address of the ICICI Branch you may use the locator. store locator.
  • Cash should be deposited in Future Bazaar India Limited - Current Account No. 039305002261
  • Cash should be deposited only using pay in slip at the branches and order number assigned to you should be clearly mentioned on the face of the deposit slip. Order Number mentioned anywhere else in the paying slip will not be accepted
